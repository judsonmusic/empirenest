Empire Nest - about

We are the owners and sole manufacturers of the Empire Nest processing center in East Java Indonesia.

We offer the finest quality of edible bird nests in several grades, shapes, sizes & color. Our state of the art processing lines guarantees perfect quality and the best possible prices.

Our bird nests are 100% pure swallow nest. We use a traditional hand cleaning technique in processing our bird nests. Although this technique is time consuming and causes a significant weight loss of the raw bird nests during the cleaning process, it guarantees that no other chemicals or additives are added during the process.

Please note that actual shape and color may vary from the picture.

Our farming and cleaning process is 100% manual, without using any artificial coloring and chemical ingredients.

Indonesia’s history in the bird's nest trade goes as far back as the 17th century. Until today, Indonesia is the biggest producer of edible bird's nest, supplying 80% of the world's bird's nest consumption, followed by Thailand 10% and Malaysia 7%.

We are a Bird Nest Wholesaler residing in the USA, and engaged in the Indonesian bird’s nest trading business for export markets, mainly to the USA.

We also accept orders from other countries in addition to the USA. We ship orders to Hong Kong, Taiwan, Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand, China, and Canada.

One of Empire Nest’s competitive advantages is the capability to manufacturer our birds nests in Indonesia while housing our warehouse in the USA. By doing this we are able to provide the most competitive wholesale bird's nest prices in the market.

Our unique position in the edible birds nest market place allows us to not only service customers across the globe, but also process our birds nests the traditional way, organic, fresh, and delicious.
